We are Legion Sugar Cookies

Who doesn't love a little sugar sometimes? Haha. Here's an easy recipe to create ~60 of your very own sugar cookies! In the picture here we have heart-shaped cookies, but yours don't have to be. These are also iced and topped with fun sprinkles, but yours don't have to be, either! Do what you want, baby!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 cups almond flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
    •

    Directions

    1. Beat sugar and softened butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
    2. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt. Cover and chill dough for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
    3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly dust a work surface with flour. Roll out dough to 1/4 - 1/2" thickness. Cut into shapes with any cookie cutter. Place cookies 1" apart on ungreased baking sheets.
    4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are lightly browned -- 6-8 minutes. Carefully transfer cookies to a wire rack and cool completely before decorating.
    5. Live deliciously!
