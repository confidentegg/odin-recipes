Dark Angel Toast

You just woke up and you're craving a light, fluffy cake. Why not take it a step further and turn that light, fluffy cake into light, fluffy french toast! Even tho this can serve 6 people, who says that you have to share? Make sure to serve with fresh fruit and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of angel food cake mix (18.25 oz)
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp butter, divided
    •

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
    2. Beat cake mix and water together in a bowl until moist; pour into a 10" tube pan.
    3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is golden brown, cracked, and looks dry (38-48min). Set aside for at least 8 hours to overnight.
    4. Whisk eggs, milk, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt together in a bowl.
    5. Cut cake into 12 slices. Dip to coat each slice into the egg mixture.
    6. Melt enough of the butter in a large skillet over medium heat to coat the pan. Cook the cake slices in batches until golden brown (2-3 minutes per side).

    Note: top with strawberries, maple syrup, powdered sugar, or whatever freaky things you're into.
